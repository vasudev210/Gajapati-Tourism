About Us


Welcome to Gajapati Tourism, your gateway to exploring the natural beauty and rich cultural heritage of Gajapati district in the eastern Indian state of Odisha.



Our mission is to promote sustainable tourism that benefits local communities, while providing visitors with unforgettable experiences that showcase the unique charm and character of Gajapati.



Our team is made up of local experts who are passionate about sharing the best of Gajapati with visitors from around the world. Whether you're interested in trekking through lush forests, discovering ancient temples and ruins, or immersing yourself in local festivals and traditions, we have the knowledge and expertise to help you create a truly unforgettable travel experience.



At Gajapati Tourism, we believe that responsible travel is the key to preserving the natural beauty and cultural richness of our region for generations to come. That's why we work closely with local communities and stakeholders to ensure that our tours and activities are sustainable and eco-friendly, and that they benefit the people and places that make Gajapati so special.



So if you're ready to discover the magic of Gajapati, we invite you to explore our website and discover all that our region has to offer. From scenic nature walks to cultural tours, from temple visits to community-based activities, we have something for every type of traveler.



Thank you for choosing Gajapati Tourism, and we look forward to welcoming you to our beautiful region soon!
